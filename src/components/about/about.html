About PMP

Why Join Project
Management at Purdue?

PMP bridges the gap between classroom theory and industry practice. As a member, you'll work directly with Fortune 500 companies and local organizations to deliver real consulting projects — building a portfolio that sets you apart before graduation.

Whether you're pursuing a CAPM certification, sharpening your leadership abilities, or exploring project management as a career, PMP gives you the tools, mentors, and hands-on experience to succeed.

📚

Industry Methods & Skills

Work on live projects using the same methodologies, tools, and frameworks that professional project managers use every day.

🎓

Professional Development

Attend speaker events with industry leaders and gain insights into the breadth of careers available in project management.

📋

Professional Certificates

Work toward Google Project Management or CAPM certifications with club resources, study groups, and peer support.
